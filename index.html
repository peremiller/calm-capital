Bear-market protocol

A pre-commitment you make in calm times: during a declared bear market you will not look at balances and will not sell. The only allowed action is your scheduled rebalance.

Normal market — balances visible
While this is on:
  • Net-worth balances are blurred app-wide (you can still log them — you just won't stare).
  • The only sanctioned move is rebalancing per your IPS rule.
  • Re-read your recoveries ledger and your IPS guardrails instead of acting.

Sanctioned action: rebalance

Rebalancing means selling what's up to buy what's down — back to your target weights. It's the disciplined opposite of panic-selling.
